Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Knot & Thread Tales ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you browse this website or place an order, and how we use it.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when placing an order, including your name, mobile number, delivery address, pincode, and any customization notes you share. We do not require you to create an account to browse or order.

3. How we use your information

  • To process and fulfill your order, including delivery and customization
  • To communicate with you about your order via WhatsApp, phone or email
  • To confirm payment and resolve any payment-related queries
  • To improve our products and customer experience

4. How orders are processed

When you submit an order form on this website, your details are used to generate a WhatsApp message that you send to us directly. We do not automatically charge any payment method — all payments are made manually via UPI, and confirmed by you sharing a transaction reference or screenshot.

5. Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your details are only used internally to fulfill your order and are shared with delivery partners solely for shipping purposes.

6. Data storage

Order details submitted through our website are stored securely and used only for order fulfillment and customer service. Product images are hosted via Cloudinary, and product data is hosted via Supabase, both of which maintain their own security standards.

7. Cookies and analytics

This website may use minimal local browser storage to remember your preferences during a session. We do not use invasive tracking or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@knotandthreadtales.com.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our contact page or WhatsApp.
