Our story

Every loop has a reason

Knot & Thread Tales began with a single crochet hook, a box of yarn, and a belief that gifts should feel like they were made for one person — because they were.

How it started

From one hook to a hundred orders

What began as gifts for friends slowly became something more. People kept asking where they could buy "the crochet thing" — and Knot & Thread Tales was born out of that question.

Today, every piece — from a crocheted bouquet to a personalized embroidery hoop — is still made the same way it started: by hand, with attention to the small details that machines tend to skip.

We work in small batches, which means waiting a little longer, but it also means your piece was made with you in mind, not assembled on a line of a thousand identical others.
